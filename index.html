About Us

Pine Hill Pizza & Grill has been serving the best pizza, steaks, and wings in the area for 24 years. Located in Pine Hill, we’ve been recognized multiple times with the “Best of South Jersey” award.

We make our dough fresh daily to ensure consistent quality and great taste every time you order. Whether you dine in or want delivery, we serve Pine Hill, Erial, and parts of Sicklerville and Berlin.

Along with pizza and steaks, our menu includes Tarantini panzerotti, salads, soups, wings, hoagies, sandwiches, burgers, wraps, stromboli, and calzones. Stop by or call us today!
